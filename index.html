About

Jet Brawley is an open source Discord Bot developed by Joe (Chee Yong Lee) and the BotStudios since Sat Dec 26 2020. The bot was mainly a multi-purpose Discord Bot, it offers plenty of features such as moderation & music. Unfortunately, in the year of 2022, Joe had a plan to shutdown Jet Brawley as it was a huge challenge to maintain the bot and keep it up to date. Meanwhile, bots like Brawly announced that they're shutting down their service, this was a downcast for the community of these games and that caused Joe to reconsider about his plan. Ultimately, Joe and his team @Bot Studios decided to rewrite the bot and made it a Supercell Games Stats Bot.

We are always happy to contribute to the community despite that we no longer enjoy playing these games.
