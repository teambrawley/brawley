About

Jet Brawley is an open source Discord Bot developed by Joe (Chee Yong Lee) and the BotStudios since Sat Dec 26 2020. The bot was mainly focused on the game Brawl Stars but soon got further and added support for Clash Royale and Clash Of Clans. Soon, moderation and many features were added but unfortunately, in the year of 2022, the owner Joe planned to shut down the bot because it was a huge challenge to maintain the bot and keep it running as well as up to date. Meanwhile, bots like Brawly had shutdown their service and many Brawl Stars player/enthusiast were very disappointed, because of this, Joe had once again reconsider and eventually put some effort on rewriting Jet Brawley and made it a Supercell Games focused bot.

Features

Jet Brawley supports the following games

Brawl Stars

Profile Stats Club Stats Club Members Stats Brawler Statistics Events Rotation Save upto one tag

Clash Royale

Profile Stats Clan Stats Clan Members Stats Save upto one tag

Clash Of Clans

Profile Stats Clan Stats Clan Members Stats Save upto one tag
